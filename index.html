Biography

I am a third-year PhD student of the Department of Engineering Physics in Tsinghua University and a member of the Lab of Institute of Technical Physics, under the supervision of Prof. Mingsheng Zhou. I got my Bachelor Degree of Engineering from the Department of Engineering Physics of Tsinghua University in June 2019. I also minored in Management of School of Economics and Management in Tsinghua University from 2016 to 2019.

My research interests lie in isotope separation, deep learning.
