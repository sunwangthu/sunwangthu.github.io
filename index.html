Biography

I am a second-year master student of the Department of Information and Communications Engineering in Tokyo Institute of Technology and a member of Shinozaki Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Shinozaki Takahiro. I got my Bachelor Degree of Science from the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Nanjing University in June 2019. I am currently a research intern at the Machine Learning Group of Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), advised by Jindong Wang.

My research interests lie in semi-supervised learning, transfer learning and imbalanced learning.
